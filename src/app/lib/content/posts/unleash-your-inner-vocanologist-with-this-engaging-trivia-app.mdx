# Unleash Your Inner Volcanologist with This Engaging Trivia App

<AnimatedParagraph>
  Are you fascinated by volcanoes? Do you think you have what it takes to be a
  volcanologist? Well, now’s your chance to find out! Introducing the **Volcano
  Trivia App** — an interactive and educational platform designed to test your
  knowledge of all things volcanic.
</AnimatedParagraph>

<AnimatedHeading level={2}>Dive Into the World of Volcanoes</AnimatedHeading>

<AnimatedParagraph>
  Volcanoes have always captivated human imagination with their raw power and
  breathtaking beauty. But how much do you really know about these geological
  wonders? With the **Volcano Trivia App**, you can put your knowledge to the
  test and discover just how much of a volcanology expert you truly are.
</AnimatedParagraph>

<AnimatedHeading level={2}>Features That Will Blow Your Mind</AnimatedHeading>

<AnimatedHeading level={3}>Choose Your Challenge</AnimatedHeading>

<AnimatedParagraph>
  The Volcano Trivia App offers two exciting game modes to suit your
  preferences. Feeling adventurous? Dive into the **“All 100 Questions”** mode
  and take on the ultimate volcanic challenge. Prefer a quick and fun quiz? Opt
  for the **“5 Random Questions”** mode and test your knowledge with a selection
  of random questions.
</AnimatedParagraph>

<AnimatedHeading level={3}>Trigger a Volcanic Eruption</AnimatedHeading>

<AnimatedParagraph>
  Beware! Answer incorrectly, and you might just set off a volcanic eruption.
  Depending on the game mode you choose, triggering an eruption requires either
  3 or 10 incorrect answers. Can you keep your cool under pressure and avoid
  disaster?
</AnimatedParagraph>

<AnimatedHeading level={3}>Explore High Scores</AnimatedHeading>

<AnimatedParagraph>
  Think you’re the ultimate volcanology ace? Check out the high scores to see
  how you stack up against other players. Compete with friends and challenge
  yourself to reach the top of the leaderboard.
</AnimatedParagraph>

<AnimatedHeading level={2}>How It Works</AnimatedHeading>

<AnimatedList>
  <AnimatedListItem>
    **Log In:** Visit the start page and log in to your account to get started.
  </AnimatedListItem>
  <AnimatedListItem>
    **Choose Your Game:** Select your preferred game mode — “All 100 Questions”
    or “5 Random Questions.”
  </AnimatedListItem>
  <AnimatedListItem>
    **Test Your Knowledge:** Answer a series of challenging questions ranging
    from easy to hard.
  </AnimatedListItem>
  <AnimatedListItem>
    **View Your Results:** After completing the quiz, view your results to see
    how well you performed.
  </AnimatedListItem>
  <AnimatedListItem>
    **Explore High Scores:** Check out the high scores to see where you rank
    among other players.
  </AnimatedListItem>
</AnimatedList>
